Cancellation Policies

For Clients
  • You can cancel a booking up to 24 hours in advance without affecting your account status.
  • Cancellations made within 24 hours may result in limited booking privileges.
  • Repeated no-shows may lead to restrictions on future bookings.
  • Exceptional cases (e.g., emergencies) will be considered individually.
  • Frequent cancellations may reduce your credibility within the community.
For Service Providers
  • Provide at least 24 hours notice when canceling a booking.
  • If canceling within 24 hours, make an effort to reschedule with the client directly.
  • Frequent cancellations may result in account review or suspension.
  • Maintain a reliable booking rate to build trust with clients.
  • Communicate and document emergencies that require last-minute cancellations.
Dispute Resolution
  • All disputes will be reviewed within 48 hours
  • Both parties must provide documentation when requested
  • SkillFlow's decision on disputes is final and binding
  • Any resolutions involving monetary matters must be handled directly between the client and the service provider.
