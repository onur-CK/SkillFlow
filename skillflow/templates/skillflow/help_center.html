Help Center

Account Management
Creating & Managing Your Account
  • To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button and follow the registration process.
  • Manage your profile by clicking on "Profile" in the navigation menu.
  • Update your personal information, including name, email, and bio in "Edit Profile".
  • For account deletion, visit "Manage Account" under the Profile menu.
Service Management
For Service Providers
  • Create new services using the "+New Post" button in the navigation bar.
  • Manage your services through the "My Services" section in your profile.
  • Set availability by clicking "Set Times" on your service listing.
  • Edit or delete services using the respective buttons on each service card.
For Clients
  • Browse services by category using the icons on the homepage.
  • Book services by clicking "Book" on any service card.
  • View and manage your bookings in the "Appointments" section.
  • Cancel bookings through your appointments dashboard if needed.
Common Issues
Troubleshooting
  • If you can't log in, ensure your username and password are correct.
  • For booking issues, check if the time slot is still available.
  • If appointments aren't showing, refresh the page or log out and back in.
  • Service listings not appearing? Verify your internet connection and try again.
